Al Fakher

Al Fakher shisha tobacco is made in the United Arab Emirates and is one of the more well known brands of tobacco in the Middle East and all over the world. This high-grade tobacco has a consistent flavor and is well known for the smoothness of its smoke. This type of shisha can be enjoyed by any level of hookah smoker.

Starbuzz

Starbuzz shisha contains French Virginia tobacco, honey, glycerin, and their proprietary blend of flavoring extracts. Their ingredients and blends are what make them one of the best shisha brands on the market.

Khalil Mamoon

Khalil Mamoon is a hookah manufacturer that has been producing handmade hookah water pipes since 1873. For hundreds of years, water pipe smoking has been a cultural activity in the Middle East and has grown in popularity in the West.

Classic Reinvented: Two Apples with Mint Flavor

Delve into the timeless taste of Two Apples with Mint, where traditional apple flavors meet a breath of minty freshness.

Two Apples with Mint hookah tobacco elevates the classic apple flavor by introducing a minty twist, offering a refreshing new take on a beloved favorite. This flavor combines the sweet and slightly tart notes of red and green apples with a cool mint undertone, providing a crisp and rejuvenating smoking experience. It's a perfect blend of nostalgia and innovation, catering to both traditionalists and those seeking a modern flavor journey.

Ideal for any gathering, Two Apples with Mint is a versatile flavor that appeals to a wide range of palates. Its balanced blend of fruity and fresh notes ensures a delightful session, making it a must-try for anyone looking to enjoy a classic flavor with a refreshing twist.

Pure Essence: Apple Flavor

Savor the pure and simple delight of our Apple flavor, a homage to the crisp, sweet essence of fresh apples.

Our Apple hookah tobacco captures the essence of freshly picked apples, delivering a clean, crisp, and naturally sweet flavor. It's a straightforward and honest tribute to the fruit's simplicity and depth, offering a smoking experience that's both refreshing and comforting. The authentic taste of apple in every puff makes it a solid choice for those who appreciate the flavors of nature without any added complexity.

Perfect for any time of day, the Apple flavor is ideal for those seeking a smooth and unadulterated taste. Its pure and fresh profile provides a satisfyingly simple smoking experience that's both familiar and comforting, making it a favorite among hookah enthusiasts who appreciate the timeless taste of apple.
